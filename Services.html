More About Us

BACKGROUND

ACN is an organization to help thousands of people to succeed through the writing of books and promoting their various talents and skills. ACN strongly believes and hope that every person will have an opportunity to believe in his/her abilities to succeed. Every person is born for greatness. The main aim of the organization is to create a platform to promote human welfare and provide the hub for individuals and corporate organizations to strive for financial empowerment, financial freedom and attainment of higher standards of living through writing and skill development. The organization strives to see other people become successful self-publishing authors and talented/skillful individuals succeeding in their careers

VISION

Committing fully to expand the capacity of writing with quality publication and providing career platforms and guidance.

MISSION

Promoting entrepreneurship and publishing of various pieces of writing and various skills and talents in Zambia.

AIM

We aim to ascend and reach greater heights of excellence by providing quality and outstanding works of modern standards through our providence of quality expertise in writing and publishing in Zambia and promoting and empowering individuals with different skills, talents and innovations.

OUR MOTTO

'Impossibility Our Possibility' This philosophy keeps ACN abreast with the ever-changing technologies in service delivery and operations

Founders

ISAAC Thewo Isaac Thewo is an author of various books. He has written a series of books called “Essential Revision Mathematics Primary Course” (Grade 5 – 7), “Essential Revision Mathematics Junior Secondary Course” (Grade 8 –9) and “Essential Revision Mathematics Senior Secondary Course” Grade 10 – 1 and Purpose Driven Mind. Isaac, who is a Co-founder and Director at the Authorship and Career Network (ACN), as well as CEO at Hanotech Enterprises, did his professional education at George Benson Christian College in Association with the University of Zambia where he obtained a Secondary Diploma in Mathematics and Religious Education. He started teaching Mathematics in 2005 and taught at various schools in Zambia. He is currently teaching at St. Mary’s Secondary School in Livingstone. He is married to Justina P. Thewo and have three sons. He is a hardworking, motivated, visionary person who has passion to inspire others especially the young people to be successful and useful in their communities

Shadreck Thewo Shadreck Thewois the graduate from The University of Zambia, Ridgeway campus, studyied Bachelor of Science in Physiotherapy (BSc PT). He is a co-founder and Director at The Authorship and Career Networ. (ACN). Shadreck Thewo is the former(2020) President for The University of Zambia Physiotherapy Student’s Association (UNZAPHYSIOSA). He is also an entrepreneur and CEO of Snow Print Limited partnering with his brother Yona Thewo. He is an Author, Life-coach and Preacher while working with various organization promoting Health, and youth innovations and empowerment.
