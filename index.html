Is an organization registered with Patents and Companies Registration Agency (PACRA) and is a member of National Arts Council of Zambia (NAC). It works with other associations in Zambia, Africa and beyond to promote writing, reading and publishing of Zambian books as well as promoting individuals who have different talents and skills. We are here to provide different career platforms and promote entrepreneurship and innovations among young people. The organization promotes two things:
1). Writing/publishing of books
2). Career and skill/ talents development.
